<p style="text-align: justify;">It’s my pleasure to welcome you to Honeyland College Magodo. Our team is committed to providing best quality education for your children. We attract children from nations and return them as leaders to nations. Our successful efforts as a school have been recognized with several prestigious awards, these are honors that recognize the commitment &amp; hard work of our staff, student and parents.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Our curriculum at HLC Magodo emphases on providing the best educational experiences in a caring and nurturing environment. We believe in motivating students to become independent and self-reliant while we discover and develop individual talent to become future leader.We impact the fear of God (which makes them word impactors).</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">We run both Nigerian and British curriculum i.e we offer SAT, TOEFL, IGCSE, IELTS, GRE, GMAT, CAMRIDGE A &amp; O LEVELS.<br />
We also offer an extensive extra-curricular program designed to supplement the regular curriculum, numerous sports teams, clubs and projects are occurring throughout the entire school year giving students the opportunity to become involved in “their” school (parent involvement is highly welcomed &amp; encouraged as parent are the primary educators of their children.We strongly believe in the importance of teachers, parents administrative collaborating and communicating openly &amp; frequently. I hope you enjoy your visit to the website &amp; should you wish to contact us or visit us, please find details at the contact page.</p>
