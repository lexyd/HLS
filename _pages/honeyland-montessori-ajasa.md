The school is an increasingly academic government approved private school established in August 2006 with the aim of “ATTRACTING CHILDREN FROM NATIONS AND RETURNING LEADERS TO THE NATIONS”.

The school curriculum covers academic talents, learners’ potentials, physical and cultural abilities among others. The school is a safe haven where pupils can take the most of school experiences as they learn everythingthey need in order to succeed. Pupils participate in extracurricular activities like Taekwondo, Ballet, Swimming, Music class and so on that suit any and all of their interests, builds lasting skills and possesses tons of school pride. It offers unique learning experiences and highly touted career advancement along with cohesive and motivated pupils that radiate school spirit long after they have graduated to the secondary schools.

The school serves a wide range of communities including Ajasa Command, Ipaja, Ikola, Meiran, AIT Road, Alagbado, Abule-Egba and its environs.

The school has everything one needs; Modern audio-visual teaching aid, well equipped ICT room, fleet of buses for effective transportation, constant power supply, well stocked library, a well-equipped health bay, restaurant (tuck shop), qualified, seasoned and dedicated teachers. Also available are dozens of recreational facilities such as see-saw, Jingle over, Lush lawns that are perfect for pick-up games or football, basketball, field for sport activities and well-equipped classrooms.

Honeyland is not just an institution, rather a way of life in realizing potentials as pupils are trained on that potential to a greater height.
